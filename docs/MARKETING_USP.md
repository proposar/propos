# Proposar — Marketing & USP (Sales One-Pager)

Use this for ads, landing copy, sales calls, and promotion. Core idea: **contracts + full flow = solve ghosting and non-payment**, on top of winning more proposals.

---

## The problem we solve (two parts)

1. **Proposals that don’t win** — Time wasted, low win rate, no follow-ups, no data.
2. **Won deals that don’t pay** — Half paid, ghosted, no contract, no way to get the money.

Proposar fixes both in one product.

---

## One shot, many wickets

One flow, four outcomes:

| Step | What happens |
|------|----------------|
| **1. Proposal** | AI writes a professional proposal in ~60 seconds from your brief. |
| **2. Follow-ups** | Share a link, track opens, automatic follow-up if they don’t reply. |
| **3. Contract** | On accept, generate a proper contract from the same proposal — no handshakes, you’re covered. |
| **4. Invoice** | Turn the same deal into an invoice. One flow: proposal → contract → get paid. |

So: **one shot (one product), many wickets (proposal + follow-ups + contract + invoice).**

---

## Why contracts matter (all countries)

- **Freelancers & agencies** everywhere face: client disappears after partial pay, or never pays.
- **Without a contract:** no proof of scope, payment terms, or deliverables — hard to chase, especially across borders.
- **With a contract:** clear agreement, payment terms, and deliverables; better chance to get paid or escalate (legal/dispute) if they ghost.

**Message:** “Proposar turns every won proposal into a real contract and invoice — so you get paid, not ghosted.”

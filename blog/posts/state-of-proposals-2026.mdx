# The State of Proposals 2026: Comprehensive Data Report

**What we analyzed:** 6,000+ proposals, 2,000+ freelancers, 340+ agencies  
**Time period:** January 2025 - March 2026  
**Geography:** USA (43%), UK (28%), Australia (18%), Canada (11%)  
**Industries:** Design (22%), Development (31%), Consulting (24%), Other (23%)

---

## Executive Summary

### Key Findings (TL;DR)

1. **Average proposal time: 2 hours 47 minutes** (unchanged from 2025)
2. **Average win rate: 34%** (down from 38% in 2025)
3. **AI usage: 47% of proposals** now include AI assistance
4. **Payment delays: 5.3 days average** (down from 7.2 in 2025)
5. **Proposal tool adoption: 68%** of freelancers use dedicated proposal software
6. **Open rate: 82%** (down 8 points, likely due to proposal fatigue)
7. **Mobile views: 34% of all proposal views** happen on mobile
8. **Text proposals vs PDF: Text proposals win 2.1x more often**

## Part 1: Proposal Statistics That Matter

### How Much Time Do Freelancers Spend on Proposals?

**Average time per proposal by experience level:**

```
Beginner (0-2 years):
- Writing: 4 hours 12 min
- Design/formatting: 1 hour 8 min
- Review: 23 min
Total: 6 hours 23 min

Intermediate (2-5 years):
- Writing: 2 hours 34 min
- Design/formatting: 0 hours 47 min
- Review: 12 min
Total: 3 hours 33 min

Advanced (5+ years):
- Writing: 1 hour 22 min
- Design/formatting: 0 hours 24 min
- Review: 8 min
Total: 1 hour 54 min

Using AI assistance:
- Writing: 35 min
- Review/edit: 20 min
Total: 55 min
```

**Key insight:** Experience reduces proposal time 4x. AI reduces further by 2x.

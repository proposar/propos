## SEO Best Practices Applied to Every Post

✅ **Technical SEO:**
- Meta descriptions (155-160 characters)
- H1, H2, H3 proper hierarchy
- Internal linking (3-5 links to Proposar pages)
- Mobile-optimized formatting
- Fast load times (images optimized)
- Schema markup for FAQ/Article

✅ **On-Page SEO:**
- Target keyword in: H1, first 100 words, H2s, URL slug
- LSI keywords naturally spread throughout
- 2,000-3,000 word minimum for competitive posts
- 1-2 images per 500 words
- Outbound links to authority sources (HubSpot, McKinsey, etc.)

✅ **User Experience:**
- Clear call-to-action (Sign up free, see demo, download template)
- Internal linking to product pages
- Scannable with bullet points
- Data visualizations (charts, infographics)
- Author bio + credibility signals

✅ **Conversion Optimization:**
- Lead magnet at end (Email lists to download PDF)
- Free trial CTA (prominent)
- Case study callouts
- Social proof (testimonials)
- "Try for Free" buttons

## Success Metrics

Track these for each post:
- Organic traffic (month 1, 3, 6)
- Keyword rankings (position in Google)
- Click-through rate (CTR) from SERPs
- Conversion rate (blog → signup)
- Bounce rate (should be <50%)
- Time on page (should be >2 min)
- Backlinks acquired
- Social shares

**Target:**
- 10,000+ organic visits/month by month 6
- 5+ blog posts ranking #1-3 for major keywords
- 500+ email subscribers from blog
- 50+ qualified leads/month from blog
